Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. How do I join the membership?

To join our membership, simply visit our Join Now page and make the payment of £10. After payment, you’ll receive a digital membership card and a unique referral link.

2. How can I refer someone to join?

Once you're a member, you will receive a unique referral link. You can share this link with your friends, and when they sign up using your link, you’ll earn a commission.

3. How much do I earn from referrals?

You will earn a commission every time someone joins using your referral link. The exact commission will be stated in your dashboard and communicated via email.

4. How do I get paid for my referrals?

Once your referral completes the sign-up, you will receive an email with a link to submit your payout details. Payments will be processed after approval of your details.

5. Can I track my earnings?

Yes! You can track your referral earnings directly from your dashboard. All your earnings and referral activity will be updated in real-time.

6. How are payments processed?

Payments are processed securely through Stripe. We will transfer your earnings to your bank account once you submit the necessary payout details via the form.

7. What payment methods are accepted?

We currently accept payments via Stripe, where you can use your debit/credit card to make the transaction. You can also receive your earnings via bank transfer once your payout details are submitted.

8. How long will it take to get paid?

Once your payout details are approved, payments are usually processed within 7-10 business days. You will receive an email confirmation once the payment has been processed.

9. Can I update my payout details later?

Yes, you can update your payout details anytime through the form link sent to your email or by contacting support at support@yourwebsite.com.

10. How do I contact support?

If you have any questions or need assistance, please reach out to our support team via email at support@yourwebsite.com. We will respond to your inquiry as quickly as possible.

11. How do I cancel my membership?

If you wish to cancel your membership, please contact us via email at support@yourwebsite.com. We will guide you through the cancellation process.

12. Can I share my referral link on social media?

Yes, you can share your referral link on social media, blogs, or any other platform to encourage more people to join. Make sure to follow the guidelines and terms of service.

13. What happens if someone cancels their membership after I refer them?

If someone cancels their membership, your referral commission will be voided. However, as long as the membership is active, you will continue to receive your commission from the referral.

14. Are there any additional fees for using Stripe?

Stripe charges a small transaction fee, but there are no hidden fees for using our membership platform. All fees are clearly outlined during payment processing.
